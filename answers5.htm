Lesson 5 - The Bible

The Bible is the textbook of Christianity. Within its pages we find the plan of God for our lives written by men who were inspired by the Holy Spirit. The Bible will show us what we need to know about our relationship with God and those around us.

  1. How is the Bible defined in the following scriptures?
    • Psalm 40:7
    • Isaiah 34:16
    • Nehemiah 8:3
    • Galatians 3:10
    • Hebrews 6:5
    • Colossians 3:16
    • Philippians 2:16
    • 2 Timothy 2:15
  2. What does 2 Timothy 3:16 tell us about the inspiration of the Bible?
  3. Who do we build our confidence in God? Romans 10:17
  4. What does Hebrews 11:6 tell us about the importance of faith on God?
